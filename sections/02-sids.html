02 — Security Identifiers (SIDs)

SID
Security identifiers
SID Structure

Every security principal (user, group, computer, service, built-in identity) has a unique SID.

S - R - I - SA1 - SA2 - ... - RID
  • SLiteral prefix
  • R (Revision)Always 1
  • I (Identifier Authority)5 = NT Authority (most accounts). 16 = Mandatory Label Authority.
  • SA (Sub-authorities)Up to 15. For domain accounts, first sub-authorities form the domain SID.
  • RID (last SA)Relative Identifier — unique within domain. 500=Administrator, 501=Guest, 512=Domain Admins, 1000+=created users.
